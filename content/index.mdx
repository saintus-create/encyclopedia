This single-volume reference offers a structured introduction to rhetoric, covering its major periods, figures, concepts, and applications. As one of the oldest and most expansive disciplines in the humanities, rhetoric engages closely with fields such as anthropology, linguistics, philosophy, and psychology.

Browse 274 entries alphabetically using the sidebar, or search directly for specific terms, figures, or concepts.

## Entry Structure

Entries are presented in four distinct formats:

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Identification** | Concise definition of a figure, term, or concept |
| **Note** | Focused explanation of a key topic |
| **Essay** | Extended analysis of a major subject |
| **Article** | Comprehensive treatment of rhetorical theory and practice |

All entries include a definition and discussion. Where applicable, entries also provide bibliographies and cross-references under **See Also**.
